What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide a better user experience. Cookies contain information that is transferred to your device's storage.

Most websites use cookies to collect information about your browsing habits. This information is used to improve the website's functionality, analyze site performance, and deliver personalized content and advertisements.

Cookie Benefits

Cookies help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences, keeping you logged in, and showing you relevant content. Without cookies, websites would not be able to offer many of the convenient features you expect.

Managing Your Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies on our website:

Our Cookie Settings

Use the cookie preference panel at the top of this page to control which types of cookies we use. Your choices will be saved and applied during future visits.

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies from all websites
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Delete existing cookies from your device
  • Set your browser to ask before accepting cookies
  • Use private/incognito browsing mode

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website. Some features may not work properly, and you may not be able to access certain areas of the site. We recommend allowing essential cookies at minimum.

Browser-Specific Instructions

Google Chrome:

  • Click the three dots menu → Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Choose your preferred cookie settings
  • You can also click the lock icon in the address bar to manage cookies for our site specifically

Mozilla Firefox:

  • Click the menu button → Options → Privacy & Security
  • Under "Cookies and Site Data" choose your preferred settings
  • Click "Manage Data" to view and delete specific cookies

Safari:

  • Safari menu → Preferences → Privacy
  • Choose your cookie and website data settings
  • Click "Manage Website Data" to view and remove specific cookies

Microsoft Edge:

  • Click the three dots menu → Settings → Cookies and site permissions
  • Click "Cookies and site data" to manage your settings
  • Use "See all cookies and site data" to manage specific cookies

Mobile Devices

iOS (Safari):

  • Settings → Safari → Privacy & Security
  • Toggle "Block All Cookies" or choose "Block Cross-Site Tracking"
  • Use "Clear History and Website Data" to remove existing cookies

Android (Chrome):

  • Chrome app → Three dots menu → Settings → Site settings → Cookies
  • Toggle cookies on or off, or choose "Block third-party cookies"
  • Go to Storage to clear existing cookies

Cookie Retention

Different cookies have different lifespans:

Session Cookies

These are temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser. They are used for essential functions like maintaining your login state during your visit.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them manually. The retention period varies:

  • Short-term (1-30 days): Affiliate tracking, recent preferences
  • Medium-term (1-6 months): Language settings, basic preferences
  • Long-term (1-2 years): Analytics data, user recognition
